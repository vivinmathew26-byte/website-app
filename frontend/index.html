Since 1995

Three decades of moving Coimbatore forward.

Mother Teresa Transport has run out of Saravanampatti for close to thirty years, building a name in local logistics one delivery at a time. What started as a small transport outfit has grown through repeat business and word of mouth — the way most trusted transporters in this city do.

The company is run today by Palaraj, and operates every day of the week, around the clock, so a booking never has to wait for business hours.
